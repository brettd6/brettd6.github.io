The San Jose Sharks

Unfortunately the last couple seasons for this NHL team haven't gone so well... After signing Erik Karlsson to an 11.5m dollar contract the team was unable to keep their captain, Joe Pavelski. Joe has been doing well on his new team in Dallas, even making the finals in 2022. At least the Sharks has a few promising rookies in Thomas Bordeleau and William Eklund, which should make 2022 interesting to watch.
